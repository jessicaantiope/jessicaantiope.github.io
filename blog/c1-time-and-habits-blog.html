Time and habit techniques

What timebox and habits techniques did you try?

Fogg method. The counterintuitive ways to be productive.

What did you find interesting?

The tedTalk by BJ Fogg about tiny habits. It was very interesting is very achievable to complete tasks by breaking them into smaller tasks. ALso i like the idea of using existing habits as a trigger to start a new habit. The article about the habits of successful people was also good and compliments the tedtalk. I liked that they suggested by starting small you can produce something great.

What worked for you?

Allowing myself less time for tasks so that i am more productive. Thinking about more small habits i could create to help me through this course.

Which will you experiment further with?

I'm quite keen to try the pomodoro technique. It seems like it's quite a structured way of time management which would be beneficial for me. I also think that i might give the meditation a go in my breaks.
