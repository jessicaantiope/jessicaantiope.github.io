What I learnt from the 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learnt that it takes quite a lot of awareness to concentrate on someone else talking without responding for 4 minutes. The other person thought it was good and having the hmm and nodding helps a lot so you are still engaging with the person.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I might start with the taking one breath a day as that seems the most achieveable. Then maybe move onto the having a buddy.

Any other musings?

Walking Meditation seems like a really good thing to do when you need to have a break from study.
